About Us

At Mega Bite, food isn’t just a meal — it’s a whole experience.

We proudly serve a fusion of bold Ghanaian flavors and global fast-food classics, creating the perfect menu for every craving. Whether you're in the mood for a juicy burger, cheesy pizza, crispy fried chicken, or local favorites like fried plantains and spicy jollof, we’ve got something to satisfy every appetite.

From campus hangouts to late-night bites, we’re here to serve hot, fresh, and flavorful meals—fast. Our restaurants are designed for comfort, our food is made with love, and our portions are always mega.

Megabite isn’t just food. It’s a vibe.
